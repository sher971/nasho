Courses We Offer

BTech

4-year undergraduate engineering degree offered in various specializations. The BTech course list includes computer science engineering,chemical engineering, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, biomedical engineering, etc.

MTech

MTech Full Form is Master of Technology. MTech duration is 2 years. It deals with existing technologies and creates new paths for research and development. This degree is offered in 4 different categories as Regular or Full Time, Sponsored, Part-time and project staff.

Post Graduate in AI

Professionals with an analytical mindset, looking to transition into the field of AI, with preferably 2+ years of formal work experience are most suited for this program.It deals with existing technologies and creates new paths for research and development.It can be done as full time or parttime
